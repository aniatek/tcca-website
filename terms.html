Terms & Conditions

Important terms for travel bookings, activities, and guest participation.

Booking and Payment

Bookings are confirmed after written acknowledgment and required deposit payments. Final balances are due prior to arrival unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Travel and Safety

Guests must follow guide instructions during all activities. Tavolo is a remote destination, and weather or transport conditions may affect schedules.

Cancellations and Changes

Cancellation terms depend on notice period and third-party transport commitments. Date changes are subject to availability and operational conditions.

Liability

Guests are responsible for obtaining suitable travel insurance, including medical evacuation cover for remote travel in Papua New Guinea.
